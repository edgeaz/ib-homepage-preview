Aesthetic Injectables & PRP in Scottsdale, AZ

Subtle, natural-looking results from a wellness-first practice. We offer Botox and Dysport, dermal fillers including Juvederm, Radiesse and Sculptra, and PRP, always alongside an honest conversation about your goals and overall health.

At Internally Balanced, aesthetic treatments are approached the same way we approach the rest of our care, with attention to the whole person and a preference for natural-looking results. We offer neuromodulators (Botox and Dysport), dermal fillers (Juvederm, Radiesse and Sculptra) and PRP, always beginning with a conversation about your goals, your facial anatomy and your overall health before recommending anything.

Our approach is conservative and unhurried. Good aesthetic work should look like a refreshed version of you, not like you've had work done. We take the time to understand what you want and to set realistic expectations together.

Neuromodulators: Botox & Dysport

Botox and Dysport are neuromodulators that temporarily relax the specific muscles responsible for dynamic lines, the creases that form when you move your face. They are commonly used to soften horizontal forehead lines, frown lines between the brows (the "11s") and crow's feet at the corners of the eyes. Both are well-established options; Dysport tends to have a slightly faster onset and can spread a little more, which we take into account when choosing what suits you. Results develop over several days and are temporary, gently fading over the following months, which is why treatments are repeated over time. Our aim is a smoother, well-rested look while keeping natural expression.

Dermal fillers: Juvederm, Radiesse & Sculptra

Dermal fillers restore volume and soften folds and hollows to bring back youthful contour. We work with several types because each does something a little different:

  • Juvederm is a hyaluronic-acid filler that adds immediate volume and smooths areas such as the cheeks, nasolabial folds and lips. Results are visible right away.
  • Radiesse is a calcium-hydroxylapatite filler that adds structure to deeper folds and contour while supporting the skin's own collagen over time.
  • Sculptra is a poly-L-lactic-acid collagen biostimulator. Rather than filling instantly, it prompts gradual, natural-looking volume over a series of sessions, with results that build over weeks and tend to last longer.

Which filler, if any, is appropriate depends entirely on your goals and anatomy, and we discuss the options, timeframes and what to expect during your consultation.

PRP (platelet-rich plasma)

PRP uses a small sample of your own blood, concentrated to isolate its platelets and growth factors, which is then applied or injected to support skin quality and a refreshed appearance. Because it comes from your own body, PRP is a natural option that pairs well with a wellness-first approach. As with any treatment, candidacy and expectations are individual, and results vary and build gradually.

How treatment works

Every visit starts with a consultation. We review your goals, relevant medical history and overall health, assess your face at rest and in motion, and talk honestly about what neuromodulators, fillers, PRP, or a combination can and cannot do for you. Nothing is assumed, a plan is individualized, conservative and made together, and you're always free to start small.

What to expect & safety

Downtime is usually minimal. Temporary redness, swelling, tenderness or bruising can occur, more often with fillers than with Botox, and we review aftercare so you know what to expect. As with any treatment, not everyone is a candidate, results vary from person to person and are temporary, and we cannot guarantee a specific outcome. If a concern would be better served by a different approach or provider, we will tell you.

Frequently asked questions

What treatments do you offer?

Neuromodulators (Botox and Dysport) to soften dynamic lines, dermal fillers (Juvederm, Radiesse and Sculptra) to restore volume and contour, and PRP to support skin quality. Any plan is tailored to your goals and anatomy.

Will I look natural or frozen?

Our goal is a refreshed, natural-looking result, not a frozen appearance. We treat conservatively and adjust based on how you respond.

Is there downtime?

Downtime is usually minimal. Temporary redness, swelling or bruising can occur, especially with fillers. We review what to expect and aftercare during your visit.
