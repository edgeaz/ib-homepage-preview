Aesthetic Services in Scottsdale, AZ

Feeling good and looking like yourself go hand in hand. Our aesthetic consultations start with a conversation about your goals and your overall health — so any guidance is thoughtful, honest and personalized.

Book a ConsultationPatient Portal

At Internally Balanced, aesthetics are approached the same way we approach the rest of our care — with attention to the whole person. How you feel about your appearance connects to confidence and well-being, and the best results come from realistic goals and an honest conversation. We take time to understand what matters to you before discussing whether any options are appropriate.

Our approach is collaborative and unhurried. We listen to your goals, review your history and consider your overall health, so any guidance we offer is grounded in your individual picture rather than a template.

What an aesthetic consultation can help with

People often come in for an aesthetic conversation when they want to:

  • Talk through appearance-related goals in a comfortable, judgment-free setting
  • Understand what is realistic for their situation
  • Get honest, personalized guidance rather than a sales pitch
  • Consider how overall wellness and appearance connect
  • Ask questions before deciding on anything
This page is general and educational. Specific services and details are confirmed during a consultation and by calling the office.

Who this may be appropriate for

An aesthetic consultation may be appropriate for adults who want thoughtful, individualized guidance about appearance-related goals within a wellness-focused practice. It may not be appropriate for everyone, and that is part of what a consultation helps determine. If a different path is a better fit for you, we will say so.

How the consultation works

A consultation begins with a conversation. We discuss your goals, your relevant medical history and your overall health, and we answer your questions. From there, we talk honestly about whether any options are appropriate for you and what would be realistic to expect. Nothing is assumed — recommendations, if any, are individualized and made together.

Options (general)

Because appropriate options depend entirely on your goals and health, we keep this page general and confirm specifics during your consultation. We do not list treatments or pricing here that have not been confirmed. To ask what is currently available, please call the office at 480‑863‑8778, and we will be glad to help.

Benefits & what to expect

People exploring aesthetic care understandably hope to feel more confident and comfortable in their own skin. What we offer is a careful, honest consultation, a plain-language conversation about what is realistic, and personalized guidance. We do not promise specific results, because responses vary from person to person. We focus on understanding your goals and setting realistic expectations together.

Safety, monitoring & limitations

Any decision about aesthetic care deserves candor. Not everyone is a candidate for every option, some options carry considerations or risks that we would review with you, and outcomes vary and cannot be guaranteed. When any plan is pursued, appropriate follow-up and clear aftercare guidance are part of the process. If a concern would be better addressed by another provider, we will say so.

Your provider

Your care is led by Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA-C, MHPE, a certified physician assistant who brings a patient-centered, education-focused approach to care. Learn more about her background and philosophy on our provider page.

Frequently asked questions

How do aesthetic consultations work here?

We begin with a conversation about your goals, your history and your overall health, then discuss whether any options are appropriate for you. Aesthetic care is individualized and never assumed.

Do I need a referral to be seen?

No referral is required to request a consultation. You can book online or call the office at 480‑863‑8778 to get started.

Which services are available?

Please call the office at 480‑863‑8778 for current availability. We share specific details during a consultation so recommendations can be matched to your goals and health.

How do you decide what is appropriate?

Any recommendation is based on your evaluation, your health history and your goals, and it is discussed openly with realistic expectations rather than promised results.
