Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA‑C, MHPE

A certified physician assistant with a patient-centered, education-focused approach to hormone health and functional medicine.

Book a ConsultationPatient Portal

Ilona Y. Bleaman is a certified physician assistant (PA‑C) who holds a Master of Health Professions Education (MHPE). At Internally Balanced in Scottsdale, Arizona, she provides personalized hormone and functional care built around listening, education and long-term partnership.

Her practice is grounded in a simple belief: patients deserve to be heard, and care should be tailored to the individual rather than a template. She takes time to understand your history, your symptoms and your goals, then works with you to build a plan that fits your life. Her background in health professions education informs the way she communicates, helping patients understand not just what is recommended, but why.

As a physician assistant, Ilona practices as part of a collaborative care team. Care at the practice is provided in coordination with the supervising physician, Dr. James Vogt, MD.

Education & training

Ilona Y. Bleaman is a certified physician assistant. Her credentials reflect the following designations:

  • PA‑C, Physician Assistant, Certified. Physician assistants complete a graduate-level PA program and practice as part of a collaborative care team.
  • MHPE, Master of Health Professions Education, a graduate degree focused on teaching and learning within the health professions.
Ilona is a certified physician assistant (PA‑C). She is not a physician and does not hold physician board certification.

Clinical focus

Ilona works with patients across a range of hormone-related and functional health concerns, including:

  • Women's hormone health
  • Men's hormone health
  • Menopause and perimenopause
  • Thyroid health
  • PCOS (polycystic ovary syndrome)
  • Medical weight loss
  • Functional medicine

Professional philosophy

Ilona believes good care starts with a real conversation. Rather than reacting to a single lab value in isolation, she looks at how symptoms fit together and how you actually feel day to day. Her goal is to help patients understand what is happening in their bodies and to make informed decisions, with realistic expectations and honest discussion of benefits, alternatives and limitations.

That education-first mindset shapes every visit. When a plan is recommended, she explains the reasoning behind it and what to expect, and she monitors and adjusts over time as your needs evolve, because care that lasts is care that adapts.

Supervising Physician: Dr. James Vogt, MD

Dr. James Vogt, MD is the supervising physician for the Internally Balanced Scottsdale office. He supports the practice's collaborative care model alongside Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA‑C.
