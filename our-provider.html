Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA‑C, MHPE

Book a ConsultationPatient Portal

Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA-C, MHPE established Internally Balanced out of her passion and deep commitment to providing quality, personalized patient care, combined with her unwavering determination to seek innovative solutions for maintaining an optimal quality of life and expanding patients' health span: the period of life spent in good health, free from the chronic diseases and disabilities of aging.

She is devoted to empowering and educating her patients and the public on the science of optimal health, minimizing risk for disease and supporting wellness across all stages of life.

"Nothing is wrong, but something is not right"

Ilona has extensive, 28-year experience in the medical field. Her career began with a dedicated focus on family medicine for 17 years. During this time, she observed that many patients would articulate their health concerns as "nothing is wrong, but something is not right." Then she faced her own personal health challenges.

Being on the other side of medicine, she recognized that mainstream medicine is very compartmentalized, and each specialty is trained to focus on and manage its own area of expertise. This approach does not consider the intricate, interconnected nature of the patient as a whole person. Ilona successfully reversed her own autoimmune condition, insulin resistance and PCOS, which served as the impetus for her to evolve her practice and expertise.

Training and approach

Ilona had the privilege of training under her mentor, Dr. Neal Rouzier, MD, an internationally acclaimed thought leader and pioneer in the field of bio-identical hormone replacement and health optimization. Dr. Rouzier's approach is firmly grounded in extensive peer-reviewed medical research and evidence-based medicine. What sets his approach apart is its comprehensive nature: it not only addresses symptom relief and quality of life, but also emphasizes the importance of replacing and balancing hormones to help mitigate the risks associated with cardiovascular, metabolic and neurological disease that occur with age.

Over the past decade, Ilona has advanced her practice and training to include functional and precision medicine. She spends time with her patients, listening to their symptoms and histories and using the latest laboratory testing and other diagnostic techniques. She addresses conditions with a combination of bioidentical hormone replacement, medications, supplements, diet and stress management, and works toward preventing future issues through personalized nutrition, diet and exercise plans for each patient.

Clinical focus

Ilona works with patients across a range of hormone-related and functional health concerns, including:

  • Women's hormone health
  • Men's hormone health
  • Menopause and perimenopause
  • Thyroid health
  • PCOS (polycystic ovary syndrome)
  • Medical weight loss
  • Functional medicine and detoxification support
  • Aesthetic injectables (Botox, Dysport and dermal fillers)

Credentials and teaching

Ilona is dedicated to her own ongoing education and to helping educate others. Her certifications and academic work include:

  • Physician Assistant, Certified (PA-C)
  • Master of Health Professions Education (MHPE)
  • Advanced Certification for BHRT through the Academy of Preventative and Innovative Medicine
  • International certification in Neurotoxins and Advanced Filler through APT Injection Training, with certification through Allergan
  • Adjunct faculty and clinical assistant professor at Midwestern University

She has been published in national peer-reviewed medical journals and presents at national conferences, podcasts and webinars on topics ranging from testosterone use in family medicine to complex cases in female patients. Ilona has served as a panelist for a monthly Journal Club webinar on hormone optimization, and on several national advisory boards regarding diabetes and the development of effective patient-education tools. She currently presents monthly webinars for the internationally acclaimed Fasting Method, a community focused on fasting developed by Dr. Jason Fung, MD, and Megan Ramos.

Education

Ilona attended Indiana University for her undergraduate studies and earned her Bachelor's in Medical Science in Physician Assistant Studies from Midwestern University in Downers Grove, Illinois. During this period she completed clinical rotations at prestigious institutions, including the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota, and MD Anderson in Houston, Texas. She later pursued her Master's in Health Professions Education at Midwestern University in Glendale, Arizona.

Beyond the office

Outside of the practice, Ilona enjoys quality time with her husband, two children and their dog. In her free time she enjoys reading, knitting, practicing hot yoga and traveling.

Primary care physician

Dr. James Vogt, MD

Dr. James Vogt serves as the primary care physician at the Internally Balanced Scottsdale, Arizona office, supporting the practice's collaborative care model alongside Ilona Y. Bleaman, PA‑C.

He completed his undergraduate studies in Michigan and attended medical school at the Rosalind Franklin School of Medicine in North Chicago, Illinois. Following medical school, he completed his residency in Appleton, Wisconsin.

Born in Michigan and raised in Wisconsin, Dr. Vogt has called Arizona home since 1994. Outside the office, he enjoys spending time outdoors playing golf, fishing and hiking.
