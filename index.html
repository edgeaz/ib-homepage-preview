Questions

Frequently asked questions

What services does Internally Balanced offer?

We focus on personalized hormone therapy for women and men, menopause and perimenopause care, thyroid and PCOS evaluation, medical weight loss, and functional medicine including nutrition and gut health. Explore the services above or contact the office to see whether a specific concern is a fit.

How do new patients get started?

The best first step is to book a consultation or call the office at 480‑863‑8778. We'll talk through your history and goals and outline next steps.

Where is the clinic located?

Internally Balanced is at 8985 East Bell Road, Suite 210, Scottsdale, Arizona 85260.

How do existing patients access the Patient Portal?

Use the Patient Portal link in the top bar, main menu, hero, Patient Resources section or footer — or go directly to the secure portal. It opens in a new tab.

How do I access the Fullscript dispensary?

Existing patients can reach our Fullscript dispensary from the Patient Resources section or the footer to order practitioner‑recommended supplements.

How do I contact the office?

Call or text 480‑863‑8778, email info@internallybalanced.com, or use the contact options in the location section below. For medical questions, existing patients should use the secure Patient Portal.
